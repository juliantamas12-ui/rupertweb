FAQ

Questions you'd reasonably ask.

Do I need a Steam account?

Yes. QuestLog is built around your Steam library — that's how it knows your games, hours, achievements, and friends. Your Steam profile needs to be set to public (we walk you through that on first sign-in).

Do you store my password?

No. We never see your Steam password. Sign-in uses Steam's public Web API with your Steam ID — that's a public number, not a credential. We don't ask for any password.

What about Xbox / PlayStation / Epic?

Steam first. Cross-store deals already cover Epic, GOG, Humble, Fanatical and 16 others, so wishlists across stores work today. Native Xbox / PSN library import is on the roadmap.

Can I cancel Pro any time?

Yes. One click in account settings. No contracts, no "call to cancel" nonsense. You keep Pro until the end of the period you've already paid for, then drop back to Free with all your data intact.

Will it work on my phone?

QuestLog is a PWA — install it from your browser and it works like a native app. Push notifications, offline shell, home-screen icon, the lot. iOS, Android and desktop all supported.

How accurate are the price alerts?

We poll 20+ stores hourly on Free, every 5 minutes on Pro. Free gets a weekly digest; Pro gets a push notification the moment a target price hits. Historic lows are tracked, so we'll flag if a current sale is the cheapest it's ever been.

Who built this?

One person, in public. Bug reports and feature requests at julian.tamas12@gmail.com get read and usually replied to the same day.
