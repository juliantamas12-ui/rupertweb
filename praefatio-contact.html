How to reach us.

Both editors share one address. We read everything that arrives and answer within seventy-two hours.

If you have received a letter from us and would prefer to reply by email, write to the address below. Quote nothing in the subject line — your name is enough. We will know.

If you would like to be considered for a later edition, or if you know someone we should be writing to, the same address works.

Press, schools, partnerships: same address. We answer all of them ourselves.

hello@thepraefatio.com

Postal correspondence will be possible from summer 2026, when we begin sending the first letters. Until then, the email is the front door.
