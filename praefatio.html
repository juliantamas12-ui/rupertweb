Letters to the people who built the world.

The Praefatio is a research project by two students, Julian and Rocco. We write hand-typed letters to the founders, investors and operators we want to learn from - asking each one a single, deeply-researched question about a turning point in their life. The replies, with permission, become a book.

Dear Mr Collison,

We are Julian and Rocco - fourteen, at school in Rome. Before writing this letter, we read everything we could about your career.

What struck us most: you were a published research scientist in your teens, then you put science down and built Stripe. We want to ask you about that pivot - what it cost, what you carried over, and whether you'd make it again.

One question. Ten minutes. A stamped envelope is enclosed.

- J & R

Specimen letter · In preparation · Not yet sent
Dear Mr Marks,

We are Julian and Rocco - fourteen, at school in Rome. Before writing this letter, we read fifty of your Oaktree memos, starting with the earliest in 1990.

The thing we kept returning to: in 2007 you wrote that you weren't worried enough about leverage. A year later, the world found out why. We want to ask you about that gap - the time between sensing something and acting on it.

When you read your own memo from 2007 today, what do you wish you had done in the months between writing it and the crisis? And what are you sensing now that you have not yet acted on?

One question. A stamped envelope enclosed.

- J & R
