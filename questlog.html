Connect your Steam account

Two steps. Takes 60 seconds. We never see your password.

1

Make your Steam profile public

Steam hides your library by default. We need three settings flipped to Public.

Open Steam privacy settings →
On that page, set ALL THREE to Public:
  • My profile
  • Game details
  • My total playtime (uncheck "Always keep my total playtime private")

⚠ The third one is the one most people miss.

2

Get your Steam ID

Two ways. The first is faster.

Option A - Vanity URL

If your Steam profile URL looks like steamcommunity.com/id/YOURNAME, paste just YOURNAME below. We'll convert it.

Option B - 17-digit number

If your URL looks like steamcommunity.com/profiles/76561..., copy the 17-digit number. Or use steamidfinder.com to find it from any profile URL.
