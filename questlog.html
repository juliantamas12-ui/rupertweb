How to sign in (read this)
  1. Make your Steam profile public. Go to Steam → Edit Profile → Privacy Settings. Set My profile, Game details, AND Owned games to Public. If any of these are Friends-only or Private, this will NOT work.
  2. Find your Steam ID. Go to steamidfinder.com, paste your profile URL, copy the 17-digit number that starts with 7656...
  3. Paste it above and hit Connect. Your library will load in a couple of seconds.

Not working? 99% of the time it's because "Owned games" privacy is still private. That's the one most people forget.
