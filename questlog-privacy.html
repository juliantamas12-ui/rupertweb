Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 April 2026

This policy explains what data QuestLog collects and how we use it. Short version: we collect very little, never sell your data, and most of what you do is stored only on your own device.

What We DON'T Collect

  • Your Steam password (we never see it; we don't ask for it)
  • Your real name (unless you type it into a public profile)
  • Your IP address beyond standard server logs (Cloudflare retains them briefly for abuse prevention)
  • Behavioural advertising data — we don't run ads
  • Cross-site tracking cookies

How We Use Your Data

  • To show you your own gaming dashboard. Your Steam ID is used to fetch public Steam data on demand each session.
  • To send notifications you opted in to. Push subscriptions are used only to deliver alerts you turned on.
  • To bill Pro subscribers. Email and payment info pass through Stripe, who handles cards. We never see your full card number.
  • To send service emails. Pro confirmation, the weekly digest if you opted in, and price drop alerts. No marketing without explicit consent.

Cookies & Local Storage

QuestLog does not use third-party advertising cookies. We use your browser's localStorage to remember your settings, Steam ID, friends list, and journal entries. If you clear your browser data, this is wiped — by design, that's the only place it lives.

Public Profile Pages

If you generate a public profile URL (rupertweb.com/u/yourname), it shows data already public on your Steam profile. You can pick a custom slug. Anyone with the URL can view it. There is no profile page until you generate one.

Your Rights

  • Disconnect at any time. Click Disconnect in the top-right. All localStorage data is wiped immediately.
  • Cancel Pro at any time. Through Stripe's billing portal.
  • Delete your push subscription. Disable notifications in QuestLog Pro Hub or in your browser settings.
  • Request data deletion. Email julian.tamas12@gmail.com. We will delete any server-side records (push subs, billing) within 14 days.
  • Under GDPR/UK GDPR/CCPA, you have rights to access, correct, port, and delete your data. Use the email above to exercise them.

Children

The Service is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

Data Retention

We retain push notification subscriptions until you disable them or unsubscribe. Pro billing records are kept for 7 years for tax purposes (Stripe handles this). We don't keep server logs of your Steam ID requests beyond standard Cloudflare retention (~24 hours).

Security

We use HTTPS everywhere. Push subscriptions use VAPID. Pro payments are processed by Stripe (PCI-DSS Level 1). We don't store passwords because we don't have any.

Changes

If we materially change this policy, we'll update the date at the top and announce on the QuestLog landing page. Active Pro subscribers will get an email.
